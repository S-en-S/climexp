Note on the ECMWF analyses

The ECMWF analyses are the operational analyses over the period for which seasonal forecasts are available. These are *not* homogeneous. In particular, the temperature in arid regions jumped up dramatically in 1997. There may be other problems in the dataset. Use with care!

I plan to replace these with ERA40 fields as soon as these are available.

Geert Jan van Oldenborgh, KNMI, 12-sep-2001
