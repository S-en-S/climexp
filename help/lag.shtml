Lag
A lagged correlation is used for when a time delay is suspected in the connection between the two variables. The sign of the time delay can point to he causality of the relationship. A correlation between two variables X and Y can mean four things:
  • X affects Y,
  • Y affects X,
  • X and Y are affcted by a third variable Z, or
  • the correlation was a coincidence.
A lagged correlation can sometimes eliminate the first or second connection.

The lag can ether be a single time delay, or a range. It is not allowed to vary both the starting month and the lag. Please contact me if you need a lag that is not in the long list.
